  • Simon Game

    Description

    Simon Game is a FreeCodeCamp Advanced Project , Which is a Game of sounds where the Player is presented to a random sequence of sounds , each sound has a color matching it so the player can see what are the sounds that are being played. the game starts off easy with just one random sound played and then the number of sounds played is increased by one each time the player guesses the sequece right, the role of the player is to replay the exact same sequence of sounds that they hear ,if they get the sequence right the level is increased by one if the player reaches level 20 they win the game. when the strict mode is on the game restarts from the beginning if the player guesses a sequence wrong !
